Neighborhood Directory and Profile

Our Homesteaders set up residency in one of our twenty-nine themed communities, based on the content of their home page. If you are interested in joining one of our communities, please read the descriptions below and choose the neighborhood whose theme corresponds to the intended theme of your home page.

For access to the neighborhod features, homesteader pages and a whole lot more, or to apply for residency, click on the name of the GeoCities neighborhood you're interested in.
